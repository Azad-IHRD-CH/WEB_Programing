Indian festivals

Diwali

Diwali, or Deepavali, is a major five-day festival of lights celebrated in India and by people worldwide, marking the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Originating from a Sanskrit word meaning "row of lights," it involves decorating homes, lighting diyas, exchanging gifts, and feasting.

Holi

Holi is an ancient Hindu festival that marks the arrival of spring and celebrates the victory of good over evil and love over hate. Also known as the "Festival of Colors," it is celebrated with great joy by throwing colored powder and water on each other, singing, dancing, and feasting.

Onam

Onam is Kerala's biggest harvest festival, celebrating the annual homecoming of the legendary King Mahabali with festivities like boat races, flower carpets (Pookalam), grand feasts (Onasadya), and various dances
